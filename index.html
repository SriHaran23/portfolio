DYORAI Overview

1. Overview of DYORAI

Definition: DYORAI stands for "Do Your Own Research Artificial Intelligence," a tool designed to automate the research process in cryptocurrency investments.
Purpose: It aims to assist investors in making informed decisions by providing comprehensive analyses without requiring extensive manual research.

2. Key Features

  • Sentimental Analysis: Evaluates the overall sentiment around a cryptocurrency on social media platforms.
  • Social Media Activity: Insights into the project's presence and engagement on social media.
  • Legitimacy Checks: Analyzes the authenticity of social media accounts linked to projects.
  • Community Verification: Determines the authenticity of communities on platforms like Telegram and Discord.
  • Fundamental Analysis: Offers insights into the project's technology, team, roadmap, and financials.
  • Tokenomics: Examines the economic aspects of the cryptocurrency, including supply and distribution.
  • Market Activity: Reviews trading volumes and trends.
  • Security Assessments: Checks contracts for security vulnerabilities.
  • Team Information: Provides details on the project's leadership.
  • Future Events: Highlights upcoming milestones that could impact the project.

3. AI Wallet Score

Functionality: Automatically analyzes a user's crypto portfolio and assigns a score based on various factors, such as token security, market activity, and community legitimacy.
Benefits: Helps investors track portfolio performance and identify potential weaknesses.

4. User Experience

Ease of Use: The tool is designed for all investor levels, requiring no technical knowledge.
Efficiency: Saves time by automating the research process, providing insights quickly.

5. Disclaimer

Non-Advisor: DYORAI is not a financial advisor; it's a research tool. Users are encouraged to conduct their own due diligence and seek independent financial advice.

6. Partnership Opportunities

Affiliate Program: Information for potential partners to join the DYORAI affiliate program.

Conclusion

DYORAI is positioned as a transformative tool for crypto investors, streamlining the research process and enabling more informed investment decisions through AI-driven analyses. It aims to reduce the burden of research, making it accessible to a broader audience while encouraging responsible investing practices.
